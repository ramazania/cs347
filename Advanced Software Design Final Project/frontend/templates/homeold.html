What's Mastermind?

Mastermind is a game all about cracking codes. Your oppenent loves making strange color-coded passwords, and its up to you to figure them out!

Guess with caution!

You only have a few chances to break your opponent's code. Choose your colors wisely, and pay attention to the hints you obtain with each guess!

Customize your
Mastermind experience!

Want Mastermind a little more to your liking? You can modify password length, your number of guesses, the available colors, and more!
