Mastermind

At the start of every game, a randomized password will be generated. Using your provided colors, your job is to guess not just the colors in the password, but their order too.

Create your password attempt by pressing any of the provided colors. A preview of your current guess is displayed above. You can change remove colors from your guess by pressing them again. Once your password is complete, the "submit" button will be available to press.

Your guess is either right or wrong. If it's right, you win! But if it's wrong, you'll have to guess again. But don't worry: hints will be given for every correct color in your guess. White Hints mean that one of your guess's colors is in the original password. Red Hints mean one of the colors is in the password and is in the correct position.
